## What Is Bizee (Formerly Incfile)?

Bizee is an online business formation service that helps entrepreneurs file LLCs, corporations, and other business entities across all 50 states. The company was originally founded in 2004 under the name Incfile and rebranded to Bizee in 2023.

The rebrand wasn't just cosmetic. Bizee expanded their service offerings beyond basic formation to include compliance tracking, a business dashboard, and tax consultation services. If you've seen older reviews referencing Incfile, they're talking about the same company -- same team, same infrastructure, updated branding and features.

With over two decades in the industry and more than a million businesses formed, Bizee has the track record to back up their claims. They've consistently positioned themselves as the budget-friendly option in the LLC formation space, and the $0 price point has made them one of the most popular choices for first-time business owners.

## Bizee Pricing Plans

Bizee keeps their pricing straightforward with two plans. No complicated three-tier structure to decode.

### Free Plan -- $0

- Articles of Organization filing
- Free registered agent service (1 year)
- Business tax consultation
- Compliance tracking dashboard

The Free plan costs nothing beyond your state's filing fee. That state fee varies -- Wyoming charges $100, Florida charges $125, California charges $70 -- and every formation service requires you to pay it. Bizee can't waive it because the fee goes directly to your state government.

### Essentials Plan -- $199/year

- Everything in the Free plan
- EIN filing
- Operating agreement
- Banking resolution
- Business email

The Essentials plan adds the documents and filings that many business owners will eventually need anyway. The operating agreement is the most notable inclusion here, since it's a document that helps establish your LLC as a legitimate, separate legal entity.

**How does Bizee's pricing compare?** At the $0 tier, Bizee includes more than most competitors. [ZenBusiness](/reviews/zenbusiness/) also offers $0 formation, but doesn't include registered agent service or compliance tracking in their Starter plan. LegalZoom's $0 Basic plan is truly bare-bones. The main trade-off is that Bizee's free plan skips the operating agreement, which [Northwest Registered Agent](/reviews/northwest-registered-agent/) includes in their $39 package.

### What About State Filing Fees?

State filing fees are separate from Bizee's service fee and are required by every formation provider. These fees go directly to your state's Secretary of State office. You can look up your specific state's filing fee on your Secretary of State's website. They typically range from $50 to $500, with most states falling between $50 and $200.

## What's Included for Free

Let me break down what the $0 plan actually gives you, because the details matter.

### Articles of Organization Filing

This is the essential service -- Bizee prepares and submits your Articles of Organization (or Certificate of Formation, depending on your state) to your state's Secretary of State. You provide the basic information about your LLC, and Bizee handles the paperwork and submission. This is the document that legally creates your LLC.

### Free Registered Agent (1 Year)

Every LLC needs a registered agent -- a person or company designated to receive legal documents, tax notices, and government correspondence on behalf of your business. Bizee includes a full year of this service at no cost.

After the first year, the registered agent service renews at a paid rate. This is a meaningful benefit, since ZenBusiness charges $199/year for registered agent service from day one, and LegalZoom charges $299/year. Getting the first year free gives you time to get your business off the ground before this recurring cost kicks in.

### Business Tax Consultation

Bizee includes a free business tax consultation, which is a genuinely useful perk that most competitors don't offer at any price tier. A qualified professional walks you through the basic tax implications of your new LLC -- things like how to handle self-employment tax, estimated quarterly payments, and whether an S-corp election might benefit you.

This isn't an ongoing service, but as a one-time resource for a new business owner, it's valuable.

### Compliance Tracking Dashboard

After your LLC is formed, Bizee gives you access to an online dashboard that tracks important deadlines and compliance requirements. This includes annual report due dates, registered agent renewal reminders, and state-specific filing obligations.

Missing compliance deadlines can result in late fees or even administrative dissolution of your LLC, so having these tracked in one place is practical. Not every $0 plan includes this -- ZenBusiness reserves their compliance features for the $199/year Pro plan.

### What's NOT Included for Free

To be clear about what you won't get at $0:

- **Operating agreement** -- Available in the Essentials plan ($199/year). You can also draft your own or use a free template online, though having one prepared by a formation service is more convenient.
- **EIN filing** -- Also in the Essentials plan. You can file for an EIN yourself for free through the [IRS website](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online). It takes about 10 minutes.
- **Privacy protection** -- Bizee does not offer privacy protection on your public filings at any plan level. Your personal address will appear on your state's public database unless you take separate steps to use a different address. This is one area where Northwest Registered Agent has a clear advantage.
- **BOI report filing** -- Available as an add-on service, not included in either plan.

## Bizee Registered Agent Service

Bizee's registered agent service is one of the stronger parts of their offering. Having it included free for the first year is a genuine cost savings, not a gimmick.

As your registered agent, Bizee will:

- Receive legal documents (lawsuits, subpoenas) on your behalf
- Accept official state correspondence and tax notices
- Forward documents to you promptly through your online dashboard
- Maintain a physical address in your state of formation for service of process

The service covers all 50 states, and Bizee has the infrastructure to handle this reliably -- they've been providing registered agent services since 2004 under the Incfile brand.

**After the first year,** you'll need to evaluate whether to renew with Bizee or switch to another provider. You're not locked in, and changing registered agents simply requires filing a form with your state. It's worth comparing renewal pricing when the time comes.

## Ease of Use & Formation Process

The formation process at Bizee is largely straightforward, but the experience has a few rough edges worth discussing.

### The Ordering Process

Getting started is simple. You select your state, choose your entity type (LLC, S-corp, C-corp, or nonprofit), and enter basic information about your business -- the LLC name, member names, management structure, and your registered agent details (which default to Bizee's own service on the Free plan).

The questionnaire itself takes about 10-15 minutes to complete, which is on par with other formation services.

**The upselling, however, is significant.** Throughout the checkout process, you'll encounter multiple screens offering add-on services -- expedited filing, compliance packages, business banking, tax filing services, and more. Each screen requires you to actively decline the offer to move forward. It's not deceptive, but it is persistent, and it slows down what should be a quick process.

If you know what you want going in and you're comfortable clicking past the upsell screens, it's manageable. But if you're a first-time business owner who isn't sure what you need, the add-on offers can create confusion and pressure.

### Filing and Processing

Bizee's standard processing time is **3 to 5 business days** from when you complete your order. This means Bizee takes a few days to prepare and submit your documents to the state. After that, actual state approval depends on your state's processing speed, which can range from a few days to several weeks.

This processing time is reasonable but not the fastest in the industry. Northwest Registered Agent processes filings the same day, and ZenBusiness offers expedited options in their paid plans. If speed is critical to you, keep this in mind.

### The Dashboard

Once your LLC is formed, Bizee's dashboard serves as your central hub for managing your business filings. You can view your formation documents, track compliance deadlines, and access any add-on services you've purchased.

The dashboard is functional and provides the information you need. That said, the interface can feel cluttered, with multiple calls-to-action for premium services mixed in with your actual account information. It's usable, but it takes a moment to find what you're looking for.

## Customer Support

Bizee offers customer support via **phone and email**. They do not currently offer live chat support, which puts them behind competitors like Northwest and ZenBusiness that offer all three channels.

In my experience, phone support was helpful for straightforward questions about filing status and plan features. Wait times were reasonable during business hours. Email responses took approximately one to two business days, which is standard for the industry.

One thing to note: Bizee's support team is knowledgeable about their own services and the formation process, but they aren't attorneys and can't provide legal advice. If you have complex legal questions about your LLC structure, you'll need to consult a qualified attorney separately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bizee the same as Incfile?

Yes. Bizee was previously known as Incfile. The company rebranded in 2023 but maintained the same team, infrastructure, and services. If you've read reviews of Incfile, they apply to the same company now operating as Bizee.

### Is Bizee really free?

Bizee's formation service is genuinely $0 -- you only pay your state's filing fee, which goes directly to your state government and cannot be waived by any formation service. The Free plan includes Articles of Organization filing, one year of registered agent service, a tax consultation, and compliance tracking. More advanced features like an operating agreement and EIN filing require the Essentials plan at $199/year.

### How long does it take to form an LLC through Bizee?

Bizee's processing time is 3 to 5 business days from when you complete your order. After Bizee submits your documents, the state's approval timeline varies -- anywhere from a few days to several weeks depending on the state.

### Does Bizee include an operating agreement?

Not in the Free plan. The operating agreement is included in the Essentials plan ($199/year). You can also create your own operating agreement using a free template or consult an attorney, but having one prepared as part of your formation package is more convenient.

### Does Bizee file my EIN?

EIN filing is included in the Essentials plan ($199/year) but not the Free plan. You can file for an EIN yourself at no cost through the IRS website. The online application takes about 10 minutes and you receive your EIN immediately.

### Does Bizee offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes. Bizee offers a money-back guarantee on their formation services.

### What happens after the free year of registered agent service?

After the first year, Bizee's registered agent service converts to a paid renewal. You'll receive notice before the renewal date. You're free to continue with Bizee, switch to a different registered agent provider, or even serve as your own registered agent (though this is generally not recommended for privacy and reliability reasons).

### Does Bizee protect my personal address?

No. Bizee does not use their own address on your public formation documents. Your personal information will appear on your state's public records. If privacy is a concern, you'll need to either use a separate business address or consider a provider like Northwest Registered Agent that offers built-in privacy protection.

### Can I use Bizee in any state?

Yes. Bizee offers LLC formation services in all 50 states. State filing fees vary, so the total cost will depend on which state you choose for your LLC.

### How does Bizee's Trustpilot rating compare to competitors?
