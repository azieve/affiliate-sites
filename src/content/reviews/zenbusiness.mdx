## What Is ZenBusiness?

ZenBusiness is an online LLC formation service founded in 2015 and based in Austin, Texas. They help entrepreneurs form LLCs, corporations, and nonprofits in all 50 states. While they're a relatively newer player compared to companies like [Northwest Registered Agent](/reviews/northwest-registered-agent/) (founded 1998) or LegalZoom (founded 2001), they've quickly grown into one of the most-used formation services available.

Their core pitch is simplicity: a streamlined process that guides you through LLC formation step by step, with affordable pricing designed to lower the barrier to starting a business. They've handled hundreds of thousands of formations since launching, and their Trustpilot presence -- 4.8 stars across 18,000+ reviews -- suggests that most customers are satisfied with the experience.

## ZenBusiness Pricing Plans

ZenBusiness uses a three-tier pricing structure. Here's what each plan includes and costs:

### Starter Plan -- $0 + State Filing Fee

The Starter plan covers the essentials:

- Articles of Organization filing with your state
- Operating agreement template
- Standard filing speed (2-3 weeks)

That's genuinely it. No registered agent, no EIN filing, no compliance alerts. The $0 price gets your LLC filed, and not much else. You'll pay your state's filing fee on top (which varies -- Wyoming is $100, California is $70, etc.), and every state charges this regardless of which formation service you use.

### Pro Plan -- $199/year + State Filing Fee

The Pro plan is where ZenBusiness starts to feel like a complete service:

- Everything in Starter
- Worry-Free Compliance (annual report filing reminders and alerts)
- EIN filing (Employer Identification Number)
- Expedited filing speed (4-6 business days)
- Banking resolution

This is the plan I'd recommend for most people. The Worry-Free Compliance feature alone is worth considering -- missing an annual report deadline can result in late fees or even administrative dissolution of your LLC. The EIN filing is convenient too, though you can always do this yourself for free through the [IRS website](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online) in about 10 minutes.

### Premium Plan -- $349/year + State Filing Fee

The Premium plan adds digital presence tools:

- Everything in Pro
- Business website
- Domain name and email address
- Customizable business website template
- Rush filing speed

In our assessment, the Premium plan is the hardest to recommend. The website builder and domain/email services are nice to have, but you can get better versions of these tools through dedicated providers. A domain costs about $12/year through a registrar, and website builders like Carrd or WordPress offer more flexibility. You're paying a significant premium for convenience rather than value.

### The Real Cost of ZenBusiness

Here's where it's important to be honest about ZenBusiness pricing. The $0 Starter plan is a genuine offer, but most people will need more than what it includes. If you want registered agent service (which every LLC legally needs), that's an additional $199/year. Combined with the Pro plan for compliance features and EIN filing, you're looking at $199/year in ongoing costs -- plus whatever your state charges.

For comparison, [Northwest Registered Agent](/reviews/northwest-registered-agent/) charges $39 upfront and includes a free year of registered agent service. Over a two-year period, the total cost comparison gets closer than the initial $0 vs. $39 difference suggests.

## What's Included (and What's Not)

Let me be specific about what each feature means and where ZenBusiness draws the line.

**Included in all plans:**
- **LLC Formation** -- ZenBusiness prepares and files your Articles of Organization with your state. This is the core service.
- **Operating Agreement Template** -- A basic operating agreement is included even in the Starter plan. This document outlines your LLC's ownership and management structure. Not every state requires one, but having an operating agreement is widely considered a best practice for liability protection.
- **BOI Report Filing** -- Beneficial Ownership Information reporting to FinCEN is included, which is a federal requirement for most LLCs.

**Not included in Starter (requires Pro or higher):**
- **Registered Agent Service** -- This is the biggest gap in the Starter plan. Every LLC needs a registered agent, and ZenBusiness charges $199/year for this. Some competitors, like Northwest and Bizee, include the first year free.
- **EIN Filing** -- Available in the Pro plan. You can also file this yourself for free.
- **Compliance Alerts** -- The Worry-Free Compliance feature is Pro plan and up. Without it, you're responsible for tracking your own annual report deadlines and state requirements.

**Not included at any tier:**
- **Privacy Protection** -- ZenBusiness does not use their own address on your public filings. Your personal information (or whatever address you provide) will appear on publicly searchable state databases. This is a notable gap. Northwest Registered Agent includes privacy protection in their $39 package, shielding your personal address from public records.

## ZenBusiness Registered Agent Service

Since every LLC is legally required to have a [registered agent](/learn/what-is-a-registered-agent/), this is an important piece of the puzzle.

ZenBusiness charges **$199/year** for registered agent service. It's not bundled into the Starter plan, which means the true minimum annual cost of maintaining an LLC through ZenBusiness is $199/year (for the registered agent alone), not $0.

The registered agent service itself is standard: ZenBusiness accepts legal documents, tax notices, and government correspondence on your behalf at a physical address in your state of formation. They scan and forward these documents to you through your online dashboard.

**How does $199/year compare?** It's in the middle of the range. LegalZoom charges $299/year for registered agent service, making ZenBusiness cheaper by comparison. But Northwest Registered Agent includes the first year free (then $125/year for renewal), and Bizee also includes a free first year. If registered agent cost is a priority, ZenBusiness isn't the most economical choice.

## Ease of Use & Formation Process

This is where ZenBusiness genuinely shines, and in our assessment, it's their strongest differentiator.

The formation process is built around a step-by-step questionnaire that walks you through every decision. You select your state, enter your LLC name, choose your management structure, and provide member information. Each step includes brief explanations of what the terms mean and why they matter.

For someone who has never formed a business entity before, this guided approach reduces confusion significantly. You don't need to know what "Articles of Organization" means before you start -- the interface explains it in context as you go.

The dashboard after formation is clean and well-organized. You can view your documents, track your filing status, and access compliance tools (if you're on the Pro plan) from a single screen. Compared to the more utilitarian dashboards at some competitors, ZenBusiness feels noticeably more modern and intuitive.

**One downside worth noting:** The checkout process includes multiple screens of add-on offers. Expedited processing, registered agent service, compliance packages, business website tools -- each gets its own screen with a sales pitch. While you can decline everything, it requires more clicking through than I'd prefer. It's not as aggressive as LegalZoom's checkout, but it's noticeably more persistent than Northwest's clean, minimal process.

The standard filing speed is 2-3 weeks, which is slower than some competitors. Northwest processes filings same-day, and Bizee typically takes 3-5 business days. If speed matters to you, you'll need to upgrade to the Pro plan (4-6 days expedited) or Premium plan (rush processing).

## Customer Support

ZenBusiness offers customer support through phone, email, and live chat. In my experience, the quality of support is solid -- representatives were knowledgeable about the formation process and could answer specific questions about state requirements.

However, phone support is limited to business hours, which is a drawback if you're working on your LLC formation in the evenings or on weekends. Email response times in my testing were within 24 hours, and live chat was available during business hours with reasonable wait times.

For comparison, Northwest Registered Agent consistently receives the highest marks for customer support in the industry, with quick access to knowledgeable staff by phone, email, or chat. If top-tier customer service is a priority, Northwest has the edge here.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is ZenBusiness legit?

Yes. ZenBusiness has been operating since 2015 and has formed hundreds of thousands of LLCs across all 50 states. They hold a 4.8-star rating on Trustpilot with over 18,000 reviews. They're a legitimate, well-established LLC formation service with a strong track record.

### Is the ZenBusiness free LLC really free?

The $0 Starter plan genuinely files your Articles of Organization at no cost beyond your state's filing fee (which every service charges and no one can waive). However, the Starter plan does not include registered agent service ($199/year), EIN filing, or compliance alerts. Most LLC owners will need at least registered agent service, making the true ongoing cost $199/year minimum.

### How much does ZenBusiness cost per year?

It depends on your plan. The Starter plan has no annual cost from ZenBusiness itself (just your state's annual fees). The Pro plan is $199/year, and the Premium plan is $349/year. If you add registered agent service to any plan, that's $199/year. Factor in your state's annual report or franchise tax fees as well.

### How long does it take ZenBusiness to form an LLC?

Standard processing (Starter plan) takes 2-3 weeks. Expedited processing (Pro plan) takes 4-6 business days. Rush processing (Premium plan) is the fastest option. After ZenBusiness submits your filing, the actual approval time depends on your state's processing speed, which ZenBusiness cannot control.

### Does ZenBusiness include a registered agent?

Registered agent service is not included in the Starter plan. It's available as an add-on for $199/year, or it may be bundled into the Pro or Premium plans depending on current promotions. Every LLC legally requires a registered agent, so this is a cost you'll need to account for.

### Does ZenBusiness offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes. ZenBusiness offers a money-back guarantee on their services. If you're unsatisfied, you can request a refund.

### Can I use ZenBusiness for a multi-member LLC?

Yes. ZenBusiness supports both single-member and multi-member LLC formations in all 50 states.

### Does ZenBusiness help with BOI reporting?

Yes. ZenBusiness includes Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) report filing with FinCEN as part of their service.

### What is ZenBusiness Worry-Free Compliance?

Worry-Free Compliance is a feature available in the Pro plan and above. It monitors your LLC's compliance deadlines -- such as annual report filings and state-specific requirements -- and sends alerts before they come due. If you miss a deadline, ZenBusiness will help you catch up. Missing compliance deadlines can result in late fees or administrative dissolution of your LLC, so this feature provides genuine value for LLC owners who want a hands-off approach to compliance.
